Cheesy Ground Beef With Rice

This is an incredibly easy and simple meal to make.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Shredded cheese of your choice
  • White Rice
  • Water

Directions

  1. Cook up 1/2 cup of white rice.
  2. Cook up ground beef on a skillet.
  3. Pour ground beef into a bowl and cooked white rice into a bowl.
  4. Stir ground beef and white rice together until an even consistency is met.
  5. Sprinkle however much shredded cheese to the top of the ground beef and let it melt.
    • The more cheese you add, the less dry the meal will be. Also consider adding tomato sauce for extra flavor and to avoid dryness.
